# 🍽️ FOODPRINTS – Zomato Restaurant Analytics & Prediction Platform

**FOODPRINTS** is a powerful and interactive restaurant analytics web app built with Streamlit, designed to analyze, visualize, and predict insights from Zomato data. From customer sentiment to revenue estimations and rating predictions — this platform helps uncover hidden patterns in the food business landscape.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📂 Upload and explore Zomato restaurant datasets
- 🧭 Advanced filters: area, cuisine, cost, rating, restaurant type
- 📊 KPI Dashboard: total orders, average rating, top restaurants
- 🍜 Top cuisines and ordering type distribution (online/offline)
- 💰 Revenue estimation based on rating × cost
- 📈 Cost vs Rating correlation chart
- 🧠 Sentiment analysis of customer reviews using NLP (TextBlob)
- ☁️ Word Cloud from customer feedback
- 📍 Interactive map with clustered restaurant markers (Folium)
- 🤖 Machine Learning model to predict restaurant ratings
- 📥 Export filtered data to CSV

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **Frontend & UI:** Streamlit
- **Data Manipulation:** Pandas, NumPy
- **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Folium, Streamlit Charts
- **NLP:** TextBlob, WordCloud
- **Machine Learning:** Scikit-learn (Random Forest Regressor)
- **Geospatial:** Folium, streamlit-folium
